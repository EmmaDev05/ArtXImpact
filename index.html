Mission

Art X Impact wants to harness the power of the arts to raise awareness and fund charities that address social issues such as domestic violence, bullying, and vengeful pornography.

We believe that by creating events that bring together victims, experts, and the community, we can promote education and understanding.

In addition, we can empower individuals and organizations to take action and make a positive impact.

Art X Impact

Art X Impact is a cultural and entertainment event with a focus on social impact.
As the first event of its kind, Art X Impact will bring together various associations from across Europe to raise awareness, address, and act on pressing social issues such as domestic violence, bullying, and revenge porn.
The event will take place every six months, in rotation in different European cities and each time in support of a different local association focused on the selected themes.
The event features a variety of activities, including panel discussions, live entertainment, workshops, art, and dance, and will feature international and national artists.
