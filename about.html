Art X Impact

At Art X Impact, we are committed to using our platform to make a real difference in the lives of those affected by social issues such as domestic violence, bullying, and revenge pornography.
We understand that one of the biggest challenges facing organizations that work to combat these issues is a lack of funding.
That's why we have made it our goal to organize events that not only raise awareness but also generate funds to support the work of these organizations.
Our events feature expert speakers and representatives from organizations working on the issues being addressed, creating a platform for education, understanding, and action.
With a strong social media presence and cutting-edge event production skills, we strive to be the leader in using the arts as a tool for social change.
Join us in our mission to make a real difference in the lives.
